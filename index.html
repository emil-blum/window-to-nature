A window into
the world outside

Window to Nature was made for the moments when you're deep in work and need a place for your eyes to rest — a view that isn't your office wall, your cluttered desk, or another browser tab.

It's a simple, full-screen window into mountains, forests, valleys and live nature streams from around the world. No ads, no distractions, no signup. Just nature.

Best experienced full-screen on a desktop, where it truly opens up like a window.
